A New Home for Graduate Jewish Life

In the coming weeks, construction begins on the brand-new Chabad Grad Student Center at 2219-2223 Carondelet Street.

This is more than a building — it is a home for Jewish graduate life.

Strategically located — three miles from Tulane Law, one mile from the Medical School, a short walk to the Warehouse District, and directly across from Anshe Sfard synagogue — this Center will become the hub of Jewish life for graduate students and young professionals throughout New Orleans

Designed with purpose, the new Center brings together spaces for learning, celebration, and hospitality:
